About Us

Sons and daughters of the Pinyin Clan united for progress

Our Mission

We are a socio-cultural organization established to bring together the sons and daughters of the Pinyin Clan, both within Cameroon and in the diaspora. Founded on the principles of unity, progress, and development, we strive to create a harmonious community that preserves our culture while fostering mutual support and growth.

Our Objectives

Foster Progress & Well-being

To promote the progress and well-being of all our members through mutual support and collaboration.

Development

To develop ourselves, the Pinyin Clan, and contribute to the progress of our fatherland.

Assist the Needy

To provide assistance to the needy within the Pinyin Clan and extend help beyond our community.

Community Sharing

To collaborate and share resources with other socio-cultural groups within the Pinyin Clan.

Our Programs

Empowering our members through comprehensive support programs

Njangi (Rotating Savings)

Build financial security through our traditional rotating savings scheme

Our rotating savings scheme allows members to contribute 50,000 FCFA monthly, with each member benefiting from the collective pool on a rotating basis. This traditional system builds financial security and trust.

  • Monthly contribution: 50,000 FCFA
  • Rotational benefits system
  • Surety-backed security

Mutual Financial Assistance

Access low-interest loans for emergencies and business ventures

Access low-interest financial assistance for emergencies, business ventures, or personal needs. Our MFA program provides flexible support with transparent terms and member-friendly rates.

  • Low interest: 1-1.5% monthly
  • Covered by member shares
  • Flexible repayment terms

Health Assistance

Financial support during prolonged hospitalization and emergencies

When members face prolonged hospitalization or sudden accidents requiring surgery, we provide financial assistance and emotional support to ease the burden during difficult times.

  • Hospitalization support
  • Emergency assistance
  • Up to 2x per njangi round

Bereavement Support

Stand together in times of loss with comprehensive support

We stand together in times of loss, providing comprehensive financial and emotional support for members dealing with the death of family members or loved ones.

  • Financial contributions
  • Mandatory attendance
  • Community presence

Celebration Support

Celebrate life's joyous moments together as a community

We celebrate life's joyous moments together - marriages, graduations, baby showers, and achievements. The community contributes to make these occasions memorable.

  • Weddings & graduations
  • Baby showers
  • Community celebration

Solidarity Fund

Supporting the needy within and beyond the Pinyin Clan

A dedicated fund to assist the needy within the Pinyin Clan and beyond. Every member benefiting from Njangi contributes 10,000 FCFA annually to this noble cause.

  • Support for the needy
  • Annual contributions
  • Community impact
